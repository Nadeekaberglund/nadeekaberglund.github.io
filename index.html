About Me

I am Nadeeka Berglund, an MBA graduate with an international academic and professional background in business administration, financial analysis, accounting, and business development. Originally from Sri Lanka, I began my career journey as a Mathematics and Science teacher before transitioning into the business field as a Business Analyst.

In 2016, I moved to Sweden to further develop my professional and academic skills. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Marketing Management, where I built a strong foundation in Basic Financial Accounting, Corporate Finance, Business Statistics, Research Methods, Supply Chain Management, Purchasing and Sales, International Marketing, and Business Digitalization. This education strengthened my understanding of financial analysis, business performance, commercial decision-making, data analysis, and strategic business management.

In 2024, I completed my Master's degree in Business Administration, focusing on Global Strategy, Managing Multinational Organizations, Global Marketing Management, Advanced Research Methods, and Corporate Entrepreneurship. My studies enhanced my ability to analyze business performance, support strategic decisions, conduct market analysis, and develop data-driven business insights.

During my Master's studies, I had the opportunity to complete an internship at Danone, where I gained valuable experience in a global FMCG environment. I supported reporting activities, analyzed business data, and created Excel-based reports using Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts to improve data visibility. I also contributed to market and commercial analysis, developed skills in structured data preparation, and gained deeper understanding of sales performance, consumer-oriented decision-making, and FMCG operations.

My interest in financial analysis and accounting grew from my belief that numbers provide valuable insights into business performance and guide effective decision-making. To strengthen my financial expertise, I further developed my knowledge in accounting, financial analysis, and ERP systems. Through my accounting studies, I completed an internship at Avesta Vatten, where I supported accounting activities including bookkeeping, account reconciliation, financial data verification, and economic follow-up. I also performed financial analysis using Excel to support accurate reporting and collaborated with stakeholders on accounting-related tasks.

Throughout my journey in Sweden, I have also developed professional Swedish language skills, allowing me to communicate effectively in a multicultural business environment.

Combining my background in education, business analysis, financial knowledge, data-driven decision-making, and continuous learning, I aim to contribute to organizations by transforming financial and business data into meaningful insights that support growth and sustainable performance.
