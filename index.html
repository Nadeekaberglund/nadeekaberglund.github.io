About Me

My Journey

I am Nadeeka Berglund, an MBA graduate with an international academic and professional background in business administration, financial analysis, accounting, and business development. Academic Background

In 2016, I moved to Sweden to further develop my professional and academic skills. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Marketing Management, where I built a strong foundation in Basic Financial Accounting, Corporate Finance, Business Statistics, Research Methods, Supply Chain Management, Purchasing and Sales, International Marketing, and Business Digitalization.

In 2024, I completed my Master's degree in Business Administration, focusing on Global Strategy, Managing Multinational Organizations, Global Marketing Management, Advanced Research Methods, and Corporate Entrepreneurship.

Professional Experience

During my Master's studies, I completed an internship at Danone, where I supported reporting activities, analyzed business data, and created Excel-based reports using Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts. I gained experience in business reporting, market analysis, sales performance, and FMCG operations.

Through my accounting studies, I completed an internship at Avesta Vatten, where I supported bookkeeping, account reconciliation, financial data verification, economic follow-up, and financial analysis using Excel.
